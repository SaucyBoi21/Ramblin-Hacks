What is the blue box that moves as I scroll through the animation?

The blue box represents our model's predicted trajectory of the soccer ball based on the player's positioning and movement by placing potential points for where the goalpost is and where the ball would end up as soon as the soccer player kicks it to the goal.

What is the virtual skeleton overlay over the soccer player?

The virtual skeleton overlay represents the key joints and movements of the soccer player's body during the kick. This visual model helps illustrate how the alignment of the legs, arms, and torso influences the ball's trajectory. By analyzing the angles and positions of these body parts, our system can better predict the direction and speed of the shot. This allows us to gain deeper insights into the player's mechanics, which are crucial for understanding the accuracy and success of their penalty kicks.
