About PK Prophet

PK Prophet is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize the world of football analytics - one penalty kick at a time. By leveraging cutting-edge machine learning, computer vision, and data-driven insights, our system predicts the trajectory and outcome of penalty kicks before they even happen.

How It Works

At the core of PK Prophet is a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) model — a form of recurrent neural network ideal for time-series predictions. Our model analyzes key visual cues such as body posture, foot placement, and contact angle just before the penalty is taken.

We use OpenCV overlays to track player body movements and goal positioning, and integrate specialized tracking software to follow the ball’s trajectory in real time.

Data Collection

Our dataset was carefully curated from publicly available footage on YouTube. Each video was manually selected, clipped, and annotated to ensure cleanliness, accuracy, and consistency. We focused on high-quality recordings with clear views of penalty kicks across different leagues and player styles.

Our Vision

PK Prophet is more than a model — it's a glimpse into the future of AI-enhanced sports. We aim to provide tools that help players, coaches, analysts, and fans understand the game more deeply. By decoding patterns in body language and movement, we unlock insights never before accessible in real-time.
