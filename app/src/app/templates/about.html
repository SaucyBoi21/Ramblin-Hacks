About PK Prophet

PK Prophet is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize the world of football analytics - one penalty kick at a time. By leveraging cutting-edge machine learning, computer vision, and data-driven insights, our system predicts the trajectory and outcome of penalty kicks before they even happen.

How It Works

At the core of PK Prophet is a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) model — a form of recurrent neural network ideal for time-series predictions. Our model analyzes key visual cues such as body posture, foot placement, and contact angle just before the penalty is taken.

We use OpenCV overlays to track player body movements and goal positioning, and integrate specialized tracking software to predict the ball’s trajectory in real time.

Data Collection

Our dataset was carefully curated from publicly available footage on YouTube. Each video was manually selected, clipped, and annotated to ensure cleanliness, accuracy, and consistency. We focused on high-quality recordings with clear views of penalty kicks across different leagues and player styles.

Our Vision

With PK Prophet, our vision is to continuously push the boundaries of AI-driven sports analysis. While our current model focuses on standardized penalty kick footage, we are actively working to expand support for varied camera angles — including broadcast-style views, overhead drone shots, and sideline perspectives.

We envision PK Prophet as a versatile tool for sports analysts seeking deeper insights, coaches who want to fine-tune technique, players aiming to sharpen their instincts, and even the entertainment industry looking to enhance sports content with predictive overlays and real-time analysis.

As we continue to train and evolve our model, our goal remains the same: to unlock the hidden patterns behind every kick — no matter the camera, setting, or skill level.
